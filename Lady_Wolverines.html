Girls' Athletic Coordinator:
Coach Gutierrez

Coach Gutierrez attended UTPA where she graduated from with a bachelor's in Kinesiology. Coach has been a proud member of the coaching staff at PSJA Memorial for 14 years. Coach has created state playoff history during her 2016-17 reign as girls' basketball head coach, going on to lead her teams to 4 consecutive state playoff qualifiers. Coach has won the Coach of the Year award on 2 consecutive occasions in 2013-14 & 2014-15. She also received the RGV All-Area Coach of the Year award in 2014, and a nomination for RGV All-Valley Coach of the Year in 2014. Coach Gutierrez is currently in her 15th season as the head coach for the girl's varsity basketball team where she aims to reignite the flame by aiming for another state playoff round qualification.
