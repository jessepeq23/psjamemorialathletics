Outcomes of Joining Powerlifting

Stronger

Who doesn’t want to be stronger? Be ready to become much stronger as you push past your limits.

Sportsmanship

Powerlifting is one of the few sports where you will have city and personal rivals cheering you on as one of their own on every lift.

Endurance

Building endurance does not mean running forever. The ability to use your heart and lungs until the job is done is real endurance.

Family

You may have heard the saying, “there is no I in team”, but here at PSJA Memorial we take that to heart. By joining, be ready to meet your new brothers and sisters.

A FEW WORDS

“Future wolverines should know coming into the sport that powerlifting combines strength, resilience, competition, teamwork, and an incredible feeling of accomplishment. I want to our athletes to graduate high school wit the capacity to regulate behavior, habits, learn leadership, communication skills, patience, and develop a resilience for when things do not go as planned. I want all my athletes to remember a quote I keep dear to myself which has motivated for many years, ‘Per aspera ad astra’, a Latin phrase meaning, ‘through hardships to the stars’.

Coach Clay
Girls Powerlifting Head Coach

“The first thing that I wasn’t ready for in powerlifting was pushing myself over my limits. Like lifting more and going on a diet for my weight class. I will say, something that helped me in my career would be my teammates, friends, family, and coaches. Being able to express to them how I feel and the bonds I’ve created with them helped me get through the tough challenges in sports instead quitting. If anything, I would tell future wolverines to always believe in yourself and have confidence. You need confidence to be the best version of yourself. Don’t be scared to try new things and make friends in competitions. Worry about yourself and what you need to do because at the end of the day you’re the person lifting the weights.”

Raylein Gaona
Varsity Athlete Class of 2023
