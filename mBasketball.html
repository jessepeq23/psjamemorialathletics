Outcomes of Joining Basketball

Faster

Being faster is something us as humans have always strived toward. As a famous quote says, “slow lions don’t eat”.

Stronger

Who doesn’t want to be stronger? Be ready to become much stronger as you push past your limits.

Endurance

Building endurance does not mean running forever. The ability to use your heart and lungs until the job is done is real endurance.

Family

You may have heard the saying, “there is no I in team”, but here at PSJA Memorial we take that to heart. By joining, be ready to meet your new brothers and sisters.

A FEW WORDS

“For students coming in to play basketball, the expectation of being a student athlete is hard work, dedication, have fun, and learn the knowledge of basketball. I want my athletes to leave with knowledge of basketball, but more importantly I want them to know that when they leave they understand the value of hard work, communication, and that whatever you put in is what you get in life. It’s just like my dad would tell me when I was y’all’s age, ‘hard work always pays off in the end’”.

Coach Wheatfall
Basketball Head Coach

”Coming into high school, I was definitely not ready for all the running and strength training that we do. High school basketball was very different from middle school. You have to be ready to work harder than you ever have. One thing that helped me out was constant communication with my coaches and always trying to improve myself. Don’t get satisfied with yourself because there’s always room for growth. One thing that I can recommend future wolverines do is make sure you make your coaches your best friends; you can count on them for anything even beyond basketball, so building that relationship early can help you for years to come.”

Leo De La Cruz
Varsity Athlete Class of 2023
