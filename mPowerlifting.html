Outcomes of Joining Powerlifting

Stronger

Who doesn’t want to be stronger? Be ready to become much stronger as you push past your limits.

Sportsmanship

A skill that many sports talk about, but here in powerlifting you will experience the most sportsmanship. Not just from your own teammates, but from everyone cheering you on.

Endurance

Building endurance does not mean running forever. The ability to use your heart and lungs until the job is done is real endurance.

Family

You may have heard the saying, “there is no I in team”, but here at PSJA Memorial we take that to heart. By joining, be ready to meet your new brothers and sisters.
