Outcomes of Joining Soccer

Stronger

Who doesn’t want to be stronger? Be ready to become much stronger as you push past your limits.

Endurance

Building endurance does not mean running forever. The ability to use your heart and lungs until the job is done is real endurance.

Faster

Being faster is something us as humans have always strived toward. As a famous quote says, “slow lions don’t eat”.

Family

You may have heard the saying, “there is no I in team”, but here at PSJA Memorial we take that to heart. By joining, be ready to meet your new brothers and sisters.

A FEW WORDS

I want incoming wolverines to know that there is a big difference between sports in middle school and high school. They need to know why they are joining, and that reason needs to be what drives their want to improve every practice. I want them to leave with a sense of accomplishment, but also with a hunger to be better than in high school. I want them to be prepared to set real-life goals and to know that failure in the future is not an option. My grandfather always told me, "if it's been done before, you can do it too". That meant to me that if I can't now, I need to find a way. Also, my high school track coach told me, "try-hards at practice, practice how to try hard at competitions".

Coach Gonzalez
Boys Soccer Head Coach

“I was not ready for the pressure, I didn't realize I had to earn my position. The importance during district is to make history for the team and reach state competition. Support from my family and close friends has always been by my side. Something I can tell the future generations of wolverines is don’t let anyone get in your head. Work on yourself inside and outside of school. Write down a goal once you’re a freshman and before every season begins, and look back at it when you feel like your motivation is being drained.”

Eleazar Garcia
Varsity Athlete Class of 2023

Culture Behind Soccer

From playing in the neighborhood streets with friends, to playing a regional playoff game in a stadium packed with fans. Soccer has the love of all its fans and players. Some cannot live without soccer in their lives, so if you're ready to put up a fight come out and show us what you're made of.
