Outcomes of Joining Tennis

Stronger

Who doesn’t want to be stronger? Be ready to become much stronger as you push past your limits.

Faster

Being faster is something us as humans have always strived toward. As a famous quote says, “slow lions don’t eat”.

Endurance

Building endurance does not mean running forever. The ability to use your heart and lungs until the job is done is real endurance.

Family

You may have heard the saying, “there is no I in team”, but here at PSJA Memorial we take that to heart. By joining, be ready to meet your new brothers and sisters.

A FEW WORDS

“I want all future Wolverines to know that the work ethic is high. You need to have a sense of responsibility and be proud to be part of this high school team. All my athletes will graduate having learned many life lessons which they will carry for the rest of their lives such as organizing time, responsibility over every aspect of their lives, and showing respect to others. If I can tell anyone a code to follow it would be the golden rule, treat others with kindness as you would want others to be kind to you.”

Coach Zepeda
Tennis Head Coach

“The competition over here is very different than how it was in middle school. There is a lot more competition here, everyone in the sport works hard, and you can see how badly they want to be the best. I wasn't prepared for the amount of time I had to dedicate to the sport, but it is something I'm glad I did. The consistency behind the hard work is all you need if you want to improve. Nothing improves your skills more than being consistent with your work and having fun. After all, it is a game, so have fun, compete with your team, and listen to your coach's advice; they really do know what they're doing.”

J Perez
Varsity Athlete Class of 2024

Culture Behind Tennis

Another secret gem at PSJA Memorial that does not get the recognition it deserves. Tennis as a sport has flourished in PSJA Memorial; under the leadership of coach Zepeda, it has become one of the best tennis programs in the area.
